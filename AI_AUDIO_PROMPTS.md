# AI Audio Generation Prompts

While this application utilizes a robust SoundFont player to provide a wide array of MIDI-based instrument sounds, SoundFonts can sometimes fall short when it comes to capturing the nuanced, organic imperfections and complex articulations of rare or culturally specific acoustic instruments (e.g., the complex scrape of a Guiro, the eerie bowing of a Waterphone, or the resonant harmonic bloom of a Singing Bowl). 

To achieve a higher level of acoustic realism and organic texture, you can use the following robust prompts with Google Gemini's audio generation capabilities (such as the Lyria model) to create authentic, dry audio samples. 

These detailed prompts are specifically engineered for AI generation: they avoid asking for effects or post-processing, and instead focus entirely on the physical interaction, material resonance, and acoustic environment to ensure a clean, usable sample.
